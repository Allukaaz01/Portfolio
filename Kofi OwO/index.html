Caffé Latte, a timeless classic

$ 20

The Caffé Latte stands as a timeless icon of coffee culture, embodying simplicity perfected. Crafted with a smooth shot of espresso and generously topped with steamed milk, it offers a creamy, balanced flavor profile that’s both mild and satisfying. The subtle bitterness of the coffee blends seamlessly with the natural sweetness of milk, resulting in a drink that is comforting, versatile, and effortlessly refined. Whether enjoyed in the quiet of the morning or as a midday pause, the latte delivers a consistent, warm experience that never goes out of style.

Matcha Latte, a refined fusion

$ 20

The Matcha Latte Macchiato is a modern twist on tradition, blending the earthy richness of premium matcha with the silky smoothness of milk. Carefully layered to create a visually striking gradient, this drink offers a delicate balance between the slightly bitter, vegetal notes of green tea and the soft creaminess that rounds it out. Light, refreshing, and subtly energizing, it appeals to those who appreciate a more refined and health-conscious alternative without sacrificing flavor or style. Every sip feels clean, smooth, and quietly luxurious.
